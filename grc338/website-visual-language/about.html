About Us

We’re a local family owned business that’s dedicated our entire lives to promoting health and wellness both for ourselves and our customers. We believe that health is wealth, and a healthy lifestyle is the most important thing you can invest in. We’re committed to serving you the highest quality juice made from the freshest ingredients, with no artificial flavors EVER, no hidden fillers, no processed ingredients or unnecessary sugars. All our juices, smoothies and açai bowls are made to order and can be customized to suit any dietary restrictions or allergies.

We take pride in the fact that locals consistently call us “the best açai bowl in San Luis Obispo.” Our açai is completely different than anything you’ve tried before. One bowl and you’ll be hooked! Come taste the difference. Our signature cold pressed juices are more than a reset—they’re a step toward transformation. Designed to support detoxification and rejuvenation, these cleanses help you optimize your health from the inside out. Whether you’re jumpstarting a wellness journey or deepening your routine, we’re here to guide and empower you.

At Whirl'd we believe in creating a sense of community around healthy living and wellness, doing our part to improve other peoples' quality of life with our products. We all want to feel well and be happy at the end of the day, so let’s do it together. We are committed to supporting local organizations, clubs, gyms, and yoga studios. We would love for you to be a part of our family too!
